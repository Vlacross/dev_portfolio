  • MTB-trail-finder

  • RSVP-event coordination platform

    (Javascript, Jquery, Express, MongooseJS, mocha/chai)

    An app for event planning and coordination. My originial inspiration for this came from trying to maintain a grasp on plans, changes, and updates to my wedding. As conversations occured in person, through text, e-mail, social media, etc, I longed for a simple single hub for communication of operations. Although this wasn't it, I had fun building this platform as it gave me a way to play around with custom schemas, user roles, and other new tools.
